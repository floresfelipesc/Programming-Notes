Follow Organizational Policies: Ensure you adhere to your organization's procedures and policies before 
starting any troubleshooting.

Identify the Problem: Talk to the user to understand the issue and gather relevant information.

Establish a Probable Cause: Based on the information, determine the most likely cause of the problem.

Plan and Implement a Solution: Develop and execute a plan to resolve the issue.

Verify Full Functionality: Confirm with the user that the problem is fully resolved.

Implement Preventative Measures: Provide guidance to prevent the issue from recurring.

Document the Process: Record the steps taken and lessons learned for future reference.
